🍫 Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 45 mins

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix all dry ingredients together.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and butter. Stir until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased pan.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes. Let it cool and serve!
